## 🧠 Overview

**Conform Object** is a Blender add-on designed to align and adapt objects to the surface of other geometry with high precision. It provides a straightforward way to project meshes onto complex shapes while maintaining structure and detail.

The addon enables users to wrap or conform objects onto curved or irregular surfaces, making it especially useful for tasks such as decals, paneling, or detailed surface placement. This reduces the need for manual adjustments when working with intricate forms.

Its workflow focuses on accuracy and control, allowing artists to fine-tune how objects interact with target surfaces. This makes it easier to achieve clean and consistent results across different models.

Conform Object is commonly used in hard surface modeling, environment design, and asset detailing workflows.
